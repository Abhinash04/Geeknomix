FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Any further questions?

What is the difference between a Track and a Course?

Tracks are career-focused programmes that include multiple courses to enhance an individual's expertise in a particular domain. Job placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connecting with top companies is exclusively provided in a track without additional cost. However, a course does not provide employment assistance and is designed for people who are looking to upskill themselves in particular areas of expertise or technologies.

Can you elaborate on the placement assistance that I will receive?

Once the you get certified, exclusive placement assistance is provided, which includes:

  1. Profile Building: Build standout LinkedIn, resume, and GitHub profiles with our career coaches, showcasing your skills to employers.
  2. Mock Interviews:Undergo mock interviews with industry experts, receiving detailed feedback on your technique and presentation to ace real interviews confidently.
  3. Connecting with top companies: We'll connect you with our partner companies for interviews, paving the way for you to secure your dream job with our full support.

Will I receive a certificate on completion of the career track?

Yes. Once you have completed all the courses and all the projects in a track, an industry recognised certificate, accredited by Wipro and Nasscom will be awarded to you. Individual certificates will also be granted for every course included in the track.

What is the mode of teaching?

We follow a multi-format mode of teaching. All concepts will be taught through videos, live classes from industry experts, reading materials, quizzes,assignments, projects, and more.

Will there be live classes?

Yes, live classes are one of the key components of our curriculum. It will be conducted by industry experts who work at leading companies. In these live classes the concepts are taught as to how it can be applied to real-life situations, thus giving a holistic understanding of the work that you are expected to perform on the job.

Are there any prerequisites for this Career Track? Is it possible to pursue this career track if I have a non-coding background?

There are no prerequisites for this Career Track. Individuals from any background can apply and learn this career track as we cover everything from 0 to 1, and help you build into a Software Developer.
You just need to have a computer/laptop with a good internet connection and need to be passionate about your career growth.

If you'd rather have a hand in everything from the front to the back, Fullstack is for you. Both options provide solid foundations for a career in software development. If you are still not sure about the specialization, you can still enroll into the Software development Career Track and our expert can guide you later and help you choose the specialization you need.

We make you able enough to secure the job of your dreams. What kind of jobs can I get in after completing the career track?

Once you successfully complete the career track and get certified, you will be eligible for job roles like Backend Developer, Software developer, Full Stack Developer, Frontend Developer, Engineering Associate and many more similar roles.

When will I get access to the content?

You will get the whole content access as soon as you complete your payment. Once you make the payment, you can access your Learning Management System (LMS) that will have all the career track content

Who is eligible for Geeknomix Career Track programs?

Geeknomix Career Tracks are open for students, graduates looking for jobs and working professionals from any educational background.

The course timing does not work for me. Can I complete the career track in my own time?

You can watch the course videos and attempt the quizzes in your own time. We usually have the live classes and the assignments on the weekend, but rest you can learn the whole career track at your own preferred timings.

Will I need a computer/laptop of my own?

Yes, you are required to have a computer/laptop with a good internet connection for this career track. It will be best suited for you to practise your skills and to attend your assignments and live classes. Minimum requirements for a computer/laptop are:

  • - Intel i3 or Above
  • - Windows 11 or Mac OS 12
  • - 4 GB RAM.

Can I access the track content on mobile?

Yes you can access the track content like videos, reading materials, quizzes, live classes on your mobile device. However certain learning tools are not supported on mobile devices that is why we recommend you to use a computer/laptop for completing the career track.
