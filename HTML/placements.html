FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Any further questions?

What type of placement assistance does your program provides?

Our program provides end-to-end placement assistance. This involves personalized career counselling, resume building, mock interview sessions, and direct connections with our extensive network of industry partners. Our goal is to empower students to secure their dream jobs upon completion of our programs.

What is the success rate of your job placement program?

We are proud of our high success rate, with a significant proportion of our students successfully placed in various companies post their upskilling journey. We attribute this to our comprehensive curriculum, industry collaborations, and a dedicated team working relentlessly towards our students' success.

What kind of companies do you partner with for job placements?

We have partnerships with a wide variety of companies, ranging from promising start-ups to established multinational corporations. Our partners span multiple industries such as IT, finance, healthcare, education, retail and more. Our aim is to provide diverse opportunities that align with our students' career goals.

Do you guarantee job placement upon completion of the course?

While no one can guarantee job placement due to varying external and internal factors, we do ensure that every student receives exhaustive placement assistance. This includes equipping our students with the necessary skills and resources to be competitive candidates in the job market. We have a strong track record of successful job placements, and we work tirelessly to continue that trend.

Are there any additional costs involved for availing the job placement services

Our job placement services are included in the overall cost of the program. There are no additional charges for career counselling, resume building, mock interviews, or any other placement-related services. We believe in providing our students with a comprehensive learning and placement experience without any hidden costs.

Can students choose the type of company or industry in which they want to be placed

We encourage our students to identify their career goals and areas of interest. We use this information to tailor our placement assistance, aiming to align students with companies and industries that suit their ambitions and skill sets. However, please note that final job placements are subject to the availability of opportunities and the specific requirements of the hiring companies.
